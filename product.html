Three Surfaces

Simple enough to trust before, during, and after a real conversation.

Cuemate is organized around three surfaces that cover the full meeting arc: prepare before, stay sharp during, and capture what matters after.

Start Session

  • Pick a meeting type — sales, demo, client review, interview, internal sync
  • Attach relevant documents
  • Get a pre-meeting brief: goal, focus areas, risks, opening framing
  • Go live — the overlay activates with your brief as context

Live — Question / Answer / Action

  • Question: what was just asked or what you missed
  • Answer: structured response shaped by meeting mode and intent
  • Action: the best next move in the conversation
  • Playbook guidance for objection, pricing, and decision moments

History

  • Every session leaves a full record — stored locally, never on a server
  • Overview, key topics, action items, decision summary
  • Follow-up draft with subject line, ready to send
  • The pre-meeting brief used for that session, preserved for reference
What It Can Do

A local-first assistant for the moments where thinking out loud is not enough.

Live Guidance Layer

Get compact live guidance that helps you stay calm, structured, and ready when the conversation turns to you.

Live STT Pipeline

Use Apple Speech or `whisper.cpp` to turn live speech into transcript-driven help and faster context recovery.

Streaming Answers

See draft responses as they form so you can start recovering before the full answer is even finished.

Pre-meeting Briefs

Before every session: a goal, focus areas, likely risks, opening framing, document highlights, and a note from your last session of the same type.

Meeting Sessions & History

Every session leaves a full local record — summary, action items, decision note, follow-up draft, and the brief that was used. Browseable in the History tab.

Local Context

Attach documents and retrieval context so guidance stays grounded in your actual material, not generic suggestions.

Open Model Choice

Heuristic-only (no network), Ollama (local inference), or OpenAI (optional, key in Keychain). Brief generation and live guidance both respect your provider choice.

Privacy Transparency

Settings shows exactly which paths are local, when OpenAI may be contacted, what is stored on disk, and where your API key lives. Nothing is hidden.
