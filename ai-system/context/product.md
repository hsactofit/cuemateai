# Product Context

## Overview

Cuemate is a privacy-first, local-first, system-level assistant for meetings, calls, demos, presentations, and other high-pressure conversations on macOS.

The core problem it addresses is the live moment when a person loses context, gets called on, and still needs to respond clearly without breaking the flow.

## Current Positioning

- Open-source AI assistant for live work
- Privacy-first and local-first by default
- Supports local models, with optional hosted-model paths where configured
- Designed to help users recover context, structure responses, and stay organized in real time
- Focused on the live moment rather than only post-meeting summaries

## Primary Use Cases

- Your name comes up in a meeting and you missed the last part of the discussion
- You need a fast summary of what is happening right now
- You need help drafting a strong response without sounding lost
- You want follow-ups, action items, and session context to stay organized around the conversation
- You want a calmer support layer during demos, presentations, and client calls

## Product Direction

- Stronger real-time guidance during live conversations
- Better privacy boundaries around recordings, transcripts, and user data
- More capable local-model support
- Agentic behavior over time for small helpful background tasks
- Multilingual and more offline-capable workflows
- A cleaner open-source contributor experience

## Buyer-Critical Product Gaps

- The live experience must feel trustworthy after a single meeting, or users will hesitate to buy.
- The product still needs a clearer signature moment that generic AI tools do not provide.
- Speaker understanding and live context recovery are central to whether the product feels real or fragile.
- The product should reduce user effort further so setup and controls do not become mental overhead.
- Role-specific value, especially for sales and client-facing meetings, is necessary for premium positioning.

## Platform Scope

- Current implementation target: macOS
- Long-term vision: a broader real-time assistant that helps people think, speak, organize, and act better during live work
